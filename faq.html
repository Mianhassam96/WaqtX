Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WaqtX.

WaqtX is an Islamic life awareness tool. Enter your date of birth and instantly see your life through Islamic history, world events, and meaningful reflection — all calculated in real time, entirely on your device.
Completely safe. WaqtX runs 100% in your browser. Your date of birth, name, location, and prayer data are only stored in your device's localStorage — never sent to any server. We have no backend, no database, no tracking.
Based on an average lifespan of 75 years. Your age ÷ 75 × 100 = your life percentage. This is an estimate — only Allah knows how much time remains. The number is meant to create awareness, not anxiety.
The Hijri conversion uses a standard astronomical algorithm accurate to approximately ±1 day. The Islamic calendar is lunar and moon-sighting based, so exact dates can vary by region.
Prayer times are fetched from the Aladhan API using your GPS coordinates and the ISNA calculation method. Times are accurate for your exact location and cached on your device for offline use.
WaqtX uses your GPS coordinates and calculates the great-circle bearing to the Kaaba (21.4225°N, 39.8262°E) using spherical trigonometry. This gives the most accurate Qibla direction for your exact location on Earth.
Both. WaqtX is built by a Muslim, for Muslims — with the intention of creating awareness about time, akhirah, and daily ibadah. The reflection prompts, mirror questions, and wake-up system are all rooted in Quran and Sunnah.
Yes. WaqtX is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Install it from your browser and it works fully offline — including all stats, tracker, Qibla (if previously loaded), and prayer times (if previously fetched).
After building your dashboard, click "Copy Link" in the Share section. This generates a URL with your DOB and name encoded — anyone who opens it will see your personal dashboard automatically. You can also download a share card image.
WaqtX supports English, Urdu (اردو), Arabic (العربية), and Roman Urdu. Switch languages using the globe icon in the navbar. Arabic Quran and Hadith text is never translated — only UI labels and reflection content are localized.
Your Growth Level (Awareness → Action → Consistency → Discipline) is calculated from your last 30 days of salah and dhikr tracking. It shows where you are in your journey and what to focus on next.
Based on your current level and recent behavior, WaqtX gives you 3 specific weekly goals — not random advice, but targets matched to where you actually are. Complete them to advance your level.
