Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

🔒 WaqtX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What data we collect

None. WaqtX does not collect any personal information. Your date of birth is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to any server.

Local storage

WaqtX uses your browser's localStorage to remember your last entered date of birth so you don't have to re-enter it. This data never leaves your device.

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser's site data, or by clicking "Start Again" in the app.

Third-party services

WaqtX loads fonts from Google Fonts and uses html2canvas (via CDN) for the download card feature. These are standard web services and do not receive your personal data.

Cookies

WaqtX does not use cookies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue or contact us via our GitHub page.
