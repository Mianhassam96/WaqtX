WaqtX shows you how much of your life you've already used — in days, heartbeats, and moments. Not to create fear, but to create focus.

What is WaqtX?

WaqtX is a life awareness tool built to make you think differently about time. Most people go through life without ever stopping to measure it. WaqtX changes that.

Enter your date of birth and instantly see your life through Islamic history, world events, and meaningful insights — all calculated in real time.

It's not a calculator. It's a reality check.

Features

⏳
Life Progress Ring
See exactly what % of your life you've used, animated in real time.
🕌
Islamic History
Your Hijri birth year, Ramadans experienced, Hajj seasons, and more.
🌍
World Events
What was happening in Pakistan and the world when you were born.
📅
Upcoming Milestones
10,000 days, 1 billion seconds, next Jumu'ah and more.
📸
Share Card
Generate a beautiful card with your life stats and share it.
📱
